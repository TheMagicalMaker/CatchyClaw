Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to play? +
Individual plays cost $1-2 per attempt depending on the machine. We offer great value packages starting at $10 for 20 tokens, with our most popular Family Fun Pack at $25 for 60 tokens plus extras.
Are the claw machines fair? +
Absolutely! Our machines are calibrated for fair play and we regularly test them. While skill is involved, we ensure everyone has a genuine chance to win prizes with proper technique and patience.
What age groups is Catchy Claw suitable for? +
Catchy Claw is perfect for all ages! We have machines with different difficulty levels and prize types suitable for toddlers through adults. Family fun for everyone!
Can I book a birthday party online? +
Yes! You can book birthday parties online or by calling any of our locations. We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance to ensure your preferred date and time.
What types of prizes can I win? +
We have a huge variety! From plush toys and fidget toys for kids, to electronics like headphones and portable speakers, gift cards, collectibles, and seasonal items. Prizes are regularly updated.
Do you offer group discounts? +
Yes! We offer discounts for groups of 10 or more, school field trips, and corporate events. Contact your preferred location for custom pricing and package options.
Is food available at your locations? +
Most of our locations have snack bars with drinks, candy, popcorn, and light snacks. Birthday party packages include food options, and outside food policies vary by location.
What safety measures do you have in place? +
Safety is our top priority! All machines are regularly inspected, our facilities are kept clean and sanitized, and staff members are always present to assist and ensure safe play for all ages.
